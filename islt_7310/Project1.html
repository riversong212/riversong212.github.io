Goal 1: Design and Development of Learning Systems

Competencies: I am able to design and develop innovative, aesthetic, effective and sustainable learning technologies based on theory-grounded research.
Goal 1: To provide students with experiences to develop theory-grounded and research-based competencies for the innovative, aesthetic, effective and sustainable design/development, and management of technologies for learning opportunities and systems.

Artifact: Introduction to Digital Media Portfolio

Objective 1.1: Students are able to design/develop learning and performance opportunities and systems including direct instruction, constructivist learning, collaborative work, and performance support.



The digital image, audio, and video in this artifact explain common types of workplace emergencies, the four elements of the Emergency Management Program, and how to develop the Emergency Action Plan according to the Occupation Safety & Health Administration using a website builder tool, Wix. I have compiled all three digital media contents into one learning module with the interactive slides created using Genially to test learners' understanding of the content in the final project page.
This course was designed based on the theory of constructivism because it involved designing and developing a learning content of my choice using multimedia production techniques in a web portfolio. The artifact addresses direct instruction by using a digital image, audio, and video to explain how to prepare for emergencies in the workplace as an employee.
One strength of this artifact is the hands-on learning of using various digital media software such as Gimp, Audacity, Screencast-O-Matic, and Genially to design learning content. Using different digital media software to create the website content was an engaging and interactive design process. One weakness of this artifact is my lack of proficiency in photoshop, audio, and video editing skills in my digital media content. Due to the time constraints of completing this artifact in an 8-week course, I could not produce the digital content with higher quality. If I were to work on this project again, I would improve the software skills needed for this project.
